## Homework grading instructions

- Within one day after the deadline of an assignment you will find a randomized order of the students in class on HuskyCT.
- Send your solutions to the student whose name appears after yours in the list. 
- Provide your feedback via email within 1 day. 

In your feedback, start by providing an overall numeric assessment of your classmate's assignment using the following scale:

* 1 = missing, or totally inadequate
* 2 = requires a major revision
* 3 = requires a minor revision
* 4 = complete and accurate solution

If revisions are necessary, provide the necessary details.

Note that your assessment will NOT affect your partner's grade! The purpose of this process is to learn from each other and develop good programming and communication skills, so please be thorough and honest, but also polite.

If your own homework requires revisions, complete them within 1 day after receiving the feedback.
